Anders Ivarsson - Spotify: Breakfast session

Hi there!


We noticed that Anders Ivarsson from Spotify was visiting Belgium next week, and kindly asked him if he would be interested in visiting idealabs.He replied instantly to our request, and confirmed he loves to contribute to our growing Startup Community. We arranged that he would give a talk during a coffee and breakfast session at Coffeelabs! We welcome these kinds of inspirational boosts with open arms, and would like to share them with you!

Andres will give a talk about Spotify, and how their startup handled growth. Going from 30 to 300 developers over 3 years, and managing to stay lean.


Join us for Andres story and enjoy a cup of coffee!
