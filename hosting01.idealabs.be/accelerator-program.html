The program in three steps

Your ambitious idea gets shaped and polished with help from our experienced entrepreneurs and knowledge partners. Shaping means developing your concept, receiving feedback on it, analyzing all information and restarting if necessary. The result? A Minimal Viable Product. Ready to be built.

Stop talking and start building. It’s our mantra – and it will become yours too. Why waste time tinkering design details when you should be building and testing and rebuilding? Our Product and IT lab is the perfect place to do just that. So get on with it. We want to see your product!

A product needs customers. Time to test it with a selected audience, analyze their feedback and go back to the drawing board to refine your product and business model. The advice from your mentors and business partners will be invaluable at this stage. Now let’s finalize and get everything ready for Demo Day.
