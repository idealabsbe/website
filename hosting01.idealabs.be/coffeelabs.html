Coffeelabs

“Add digital to entrepreneurialism and mix in a little coffee”

WHAT TO EXPECT?

A place where an IT coder, an investor and a professor meet around for coffee and start exchanging ideas and start building something.

On the ground floor of our Entrepreneurial HUB you will find Coffeelabs. A vibrant place that brings together entrepreneurs, innovators, creatives and caffeine heads. Our knowledgeable barista will guide you through our different selection of coffees and home-made ice teas. Besides delicious coffee, homemade hot-pressed sandwiches, specialities of the day and a selection baked goods will be served.

Next to our daily offer of coffee and food, join us for some coworking at meet likeminded people!
