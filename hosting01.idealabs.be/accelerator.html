Shape,build,and go to market in three months

#Stoptalkingstartbuilding

“At idealabs, your idea gets a launching pad. You’re provided with seed capital, top mentors, enterprise-level guidance, a great infrastructure and a heavy boot camp to get your project off the ground and accelerate innovation. Now let’s grow!”

A little detail

idealabs is a unique start up accelerator program, operating from the creative city of Antwerp. We make sure promising and innovative ideas get transformed into real products and services, and make it to the market. Our three-month bootcamp measurably increases your chances of success. In this period, idealabs will be your Energizer bunny, providing power to make your business run. From business plan validation through development of a market-testable Minimal Viable Product (MVP) to funding and scaling.

Why do we exist?

Most ideas never launch. The main reasons are:

  • lack of funding at very early stage
  • limited access to great people to form a team
  • lack of expertise to build, grow and scale

We think that’s a real problem. So we will help you overcome these three hurdles.

What do you get?

  • 15,000€ - Seed capital to build a Minimal Viable Product (MVP) and validate business plan assumptions
  • Follow-up funding for successful ventures
  • High-level business services in legal, HR and fiscal matters
  • Invaluable business training and preparation
  • Mentoring by experienced entrepreneurs
  • Convenient office space and facilities
  • Entrepreneurial classes, seminars, matchmaking events

idealabs launches and grows you startup every step of the way in return for a 10 percent stake in the business.
